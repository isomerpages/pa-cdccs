<p><strong>Jobs Nearby @ CDC’ is a</strong>  <strong>nation-wide job matching initiative by the five CDCs</strong> to
connect residents (Singaporeans and PRs) with job opportunities. These
job opportunities include full-time/part-time/flexible Jobs/PMET /Non-PMET,
nearer their homes and supports heartland businesses with their manpower
needs.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3><strong>Jobs Nearby @ CDC’</strong> has three key components:</h3>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Job Ambassadors –</strong> Offer residents with personalised job
matching support and end-to-end guidance throughout their job search journey</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>District Job Banks –</strong> One-stop platform to connect residents
with various job opportunities (ranging from blue to white-collar jobs
and part-time/flexible options)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Job Clinics</strong> – Conveniently located within the neighbourhood
to facilitate the job search and support process.</p>
</li>
</ol>

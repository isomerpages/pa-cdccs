<p><strong><a href="/programmes/socio-emotional-wellness/purple-outings" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Purple Outings</a></strong>
<br>Purple Outings is a volunteer-driven inclusive initiative to engage Persons
with Disabilities (PWDs). The programme typically involves curating excursions
to allow PWDs and volunteers to spend quality time together. This is to
foster awareness for PWD communities and help build meaningful relationships
with partners and volunteers.

<p><strong><a href="/programmes/socio-emotional-wellness/sole-mates" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Sole Mates</a></strong>
<br>Sole Mates provides support to empower partners and Persons with Disabilities
(PWDs) to keep fit with a buddy through regular exercise activities, races,
competitions. Through Sole Mates, PWDs are given an opportunity to not
only pursue an active lifestyle but also forge strong friendships within
our community, fostering a more inclusive society in Singapore through
sports.

<p>Better Homes @ Central is an initiative to provide families with school-going
children and youths with a better living and learning environment.</p>
<div class="iframe-wrapper">
<iframe referrerpolicy="strict-origin-when-cross-origin" height="315" width="560" allowfullscreen="true" frameborder="0" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/0ab3xX-3PEE"></iframe>
</div>
<p>The programme is designed to provide one-off home refurbishment to support
families with children staying in public rental flats within Central district.</p>
<p>The makeover process includes:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>Consultation between designers and families to co-create living spaces
that suit their lifestyle and needs</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Engagement of contractors, movers, and cleaners to refurbish the homes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Provision of basic furnishings such as study tables, beds, and wardrobes</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Sharing of home organising tips by professionals</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ul>

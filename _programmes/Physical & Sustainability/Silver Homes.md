<p>Silver Homes is a volunteer-driven initiative to create healthy, safe
and clean living environments for disadvantaged seniors. We hope to improve
the mental and physical well-being of seniors through home improvement
initiatives such as:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Spring cleaning</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Painting</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pest treatment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Replacement of bedframes and mattress sets</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Volunteer with us!</strong>
<br>Interested organisations or volunteer groups with at least 25 volunteers
aged 16 and above may contact the CDC to help refurbish our seniors' homes.</p>

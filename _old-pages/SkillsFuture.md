<h2>WHAT CAN YOU EXPECT?</h2>
<p>• Learn what to look out for as you embark in your own journey of skills
and career development.
<br>• Be exposed to first-hand knowledge of the latest global and local trending
skills in the workforce.
<br>• Be empowered with the resources and tools to navigate our skills-based
industry.
<br>• Meet like-minded keen learners in their pursuits to be ‘Future Ready’!

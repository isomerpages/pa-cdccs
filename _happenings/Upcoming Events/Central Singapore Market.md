Held on a monthly basis, Central Singapore Market is a platform to encourage healthy eating habits among our residents. By bringing fresh, organic produce to the heartlands, we hope to build a community of like-minded folks who encourage each other to maintain a healthy lifestyle. Head down to our upcoming markets to find out more!
